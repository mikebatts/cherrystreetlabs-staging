Our promise

Clear scope, honest partnership.
No surprises.

Every engagement starts with a conversation about what you're building and how we can help. Sprint and Build projects come with a signed scope document — exact features, platforms, integrations, and success metrics. You'll always know what you're paying for before we start.

If scope changes

Included in every Sprint and Build contract.

The scope is fixed for the agreement we sign together. If your needs change mid-project, we'll document a change order first — no surprised invoices. Or, we can transition the project to our monthly Subscription model so we keep building without renegotiating. Either way, everything is discussed and agreed upfront.

Subscription clients: The scope evolves month to month. Each cycle we plan together, ship what matters most, and adjust. You're not locked into a spec — you've got a product partner.

External accounts: For services like Stripe, OpenAI, or Supabase, we walk you through setup during kickoff. You own the accounts and payment methods — we just connect to them.
